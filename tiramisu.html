Tiramisu

Preparation Time: 4 hours (including chilling)

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups strong coffee, cooled
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese.
  3. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer in a dish.
  4. Spread mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers.
  5. Repeat layers and dust with cocoa powder.
  6. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
