Spaghetti Bolognese

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti.
