Tacos al Pastor

Preparation Time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp achiote paste
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 pineapple, sliced
  • Corn tortillas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate pork with achiote paste and chili powder for 1 hour.
  2. Grill pork and pineapple slices until cooked.
  3. Chop grilled pork and pineapple into small pieces.
  4. Serve on warm tortillas with onions and cilantro.
